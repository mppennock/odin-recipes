The BEST Coffee

Description

Do you want to feel alive? Taste life to the fullest? Go get your beans and prepare yourself for the best day of your life.

Ingredients

  • your favorite beans
  • some water
  • a french press
  • excitement (the secret ingredient)

Now What?

  1. Get excited
  2. Boil that water
  3. Grind those beans
  4. Put grounds in French press
  5. Pour hot water over grounds and wait. Patience, young grasshopper!
  6. Press down with gentleness, please.
  7. Pour that liquid gold into your favorite mug!

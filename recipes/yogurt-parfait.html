Yogurt Parfait

Description

A cup of perfection: creamy yogurt, fresh fruit, crunchy granola, and other yummy toppings

Ingredients

  • Plain greek yogurt
  • Honey
  • Fruit of choice (raspberries, blueberries, grapes, banana slices...)
  • Granola (homemade is best!)
  • Other toppings (chocolate chips, nuts, etc.)

Directions

  1. Dollop a few spoonfuls of yogurt into a glass
  2. Add half of fruit and granola and toppings
  3. Dollop more spoonfuls of yogurt into that glass
  4. Add the rest of fruit and granola and toppings

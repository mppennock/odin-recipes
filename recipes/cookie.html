Cookies

Description

The most delicious, moist, and chocolate-y cookies you will ever taste. Very easy to make with ingredients you likely already have in your cupboard.

In less than an hour, you will have 2 dozen amazing bites of heaven!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon basking soda
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups chocolate chips

Steps to Heaven

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar and brown sugar
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time
  4. Stir in the vanilla
  5. Add baking soda
  6. Stir in flour and chocolate chips
  7. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans
  8. Bake for 10 minutes or so and voila!
